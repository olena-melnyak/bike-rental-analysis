## 🔍 Key Insights

- 📈 Rentals increase in warmer months  
- 🌡 Higher temperature → more rentals  
- 💧 High humidity reduces demand  
- 📅 Demand varies by time and day

## 💡 Conclusion

👉 Weather and season strongly affect bike demand
